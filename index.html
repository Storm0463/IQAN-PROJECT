Important Notice

The Institute of Quality Assurance of Nigeria (IQAN) reserves the right to publicly disclaim, suspend, withdraw recognition from, or terminate affiliations with any member, organization, institution, partner or representative found to have violated the institute’s ethical standards, professional conduct requirements, partnership agreements, quality principles or operational policies.

IQAN is committed to maintaining integrity, accountability, professionalism and quality culture in all its activities, partnerships and representations.
